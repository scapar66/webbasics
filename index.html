Looney Tunes is a series of performances by anthropomorphized actors. This is routinely re-established through humorous fourth-wall breaks, and hard resets to the world after each curtain.

Bugs performs gender. When he is in drag, his gender presentation is seldom the punchline -- it is, instead, his endless wit that brings the crowd to their knees.

Here are some instances of Bugs Bunny's genderbending antics.

Episode Name Release Year Description
Hare-um Scare-um 1939 Bugs dresses up as a female dog to spoof the hunting dog.
Elmer's Pet Rabbit 1941 Bugs dances with Fudd and adresses him saying, Katherine-Hepburn-like, "You dance divinely, really you do."
