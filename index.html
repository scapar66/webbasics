Looney Tunes is a series of performances by anthropomorphized actors. This is routinely re-established through humorous fourth-wall breaks, and hard resets to the world after each curtain.

Bugs performs gender. When he is in drag, his gender presentation is seldom the punchline -- it is, instead, his wit and charisma that bring the crowd to their knees.

Here are some instances of Bugs Bunny's genderbending antics.

Episode Name
Year
Description
Hare-um Scare-um

Bugs dresses up as a female dog to spoof the hunting dog.

The Heckling Hare

The dog, believing that he killed Bugs, lays flowers next to his home, and Bugs takes the dog's flowers coquetteishly, saying "For me? Oh, you darling!"
The Wabbit Who Came to Supper

After shaving his face and underarms, a puff powdered Bugs appears in women's lingerie and screams as Fudd opens the door on him.
Bugs Bunny Gets the Boid
1942
Bugs dances with Beaky Buzzard and asks "Why don't we do this more often?". The bird also interrupts him mid-shower, and Bugs coyly replies "You naughty, naughty boy!"
